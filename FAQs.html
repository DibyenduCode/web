Q1. Is the J.R.D. Tata Memorial Library open to the public?

General public are welcome to visit the library, and take a library tour. They can also view the collection, but referencing or borrowing of documents are not allowed.

Q2. How can one become a member of J.R.D. Tata Memorial library?

IISc Student/Staff can walk into the Library's circulation counter on any of the working days between 9.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M. along with their ID cards and can enroll themselves as a library members.

Q3. How many books can a library member from IISc borrow from the library?

UG Students - 2 Documents ; PG Students - 4 Documents ; Faculty - 6 Documents
